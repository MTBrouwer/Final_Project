Mission Statement

CCSU's eSports and Gaming Club strives to create an environment that welcomes both competitive and casual players in one club. While hosting some teams that compete in tournaments throughout each semester, we also host events on campus where students are encouraged to join and have some fun with like minded students. Meetings are held every other weekend where members are invited to communicate on the topics of what kind of event the members would like to see in the future where they are then voted upon. With the creation of the eSports room, this allows the access of our club to spread out to a wider range of students on campus that would have otherwise not previously known of the club. Each member's opinion in the club is important as they are what makes the club so succesful every semester.
